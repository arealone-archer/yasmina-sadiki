Despite Yasmina Sadiki’s humble discography, she is a veteran within Sydney’s live music scene. This independent artist who is returning from her sold out show at The Sydney Opera House, has been pioneering a genre that has no boundaries. Yasmina shares truthful art that contemplates womanhood, love and philosophies that are drawn from her own life, she is a genuine visionary. Her captivating performances are never the same and all who witness the magic know it to be a seductively cinematic experience.
